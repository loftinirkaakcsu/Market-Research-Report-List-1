<p><p>The dental extraoral X-ray market comprises primarily two types: panoramic and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT). Panoramic X-rays provide a comprehensive, two-dimensional view of the entire mouth, including teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning. In contrast, CBCT offers three-dimensional imaging, allowing for enhanced visualization of complex dental anatomy, such as impacted teeth and bone structure, which significantly aids in surgical planning and evaluation. Both types are essential in modern dental practices.</p></p>
